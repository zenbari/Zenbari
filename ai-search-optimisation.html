The Shift

How Search Is Changing

For years, searching online meant typing a query and scrolling through a list of blue links. That's changing. AI-powered search experiences increasingly generate direct answers, summarising information from across the web rather than simply listing where to find it.

This doesn't mean traditional search is disappearing — most people still use it every day. But a growing share of research and buying journeys now happen through AI assistants and AI-generated answers, which read and interpret content differently to a traditional search engine.

What Is AI Search Optimisation?

AI Search Optimisation is the practice of structuring your website and content so AI-powered tools can understand, trust and accurately represent your business. It sits alongside traditional SEO rather than replacing it, and is usually discussed using two terms.

GEO

Generative Engine Optimisation

The practice of structuring content so AI tools that generate written answers — summarising information from multiple sources — can accurately understand and reference your business when forming a response.

AEO

Answer Engine Optimisation

The practice of structuring content so it can be easily extracted as a direct answer to a specific question, whether that question is typed into a search bar or spoken to a voice assistant.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Search Optimisation?

AI Search Optimisation is the practice of structuring your website and content so AI-powered search tools can understand, trust and accurately represent your business, alongside your existing SEO work.

What is GEO?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimisation — structuring content so AI tools that generate summarised answers from multiple sources can accurately understand and reference your business.

What is AEO?

AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimisation — structuring content so it can be easily extracted as a direct answer to a specific question, whether typed or spoken to a voice assistant.

How is this different from SEO?

Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in a list of search results. AI Search Optimisation focuses on how your content is understood, summarised and referenced within AI-generated answers. The two work together rather than replacing one another.

Does AI Search replace Google?

No. Traditional search engines remain a major part of how people find information online. AI-powered search experiences are growing alongside them, which is why businesses benefit from being prepared for both.

Is this suitable for small businesses?

Yes. The foundations of AI Search Optimisation — clear content, accurate information and good structure — benefit businesses of any size, and are often more achievable for smaller, focused websites.
