Common Pitfalls

Why Most Business Websites Don't Generate Enquiries

Most businesses don't have a website problem — they have a performance problem. The site exists, looks reasonable, and still fails to bring in enquiries.

The reasons are rarely technical. They're almost always about speed, experience, trust and how clearly the site guides a visitor toward taking action.

  • Slow Performance

    Every extra second of load time pushes visitors away before they even see what you offer.

  • Poor User Experience

    Confusing navigation and cluttered layouts make it hard for visitors to find what they came for.

  • Weak Messaging

    Generic, vague copy fails to explain why a visitor should choose your business over anyone else.

  • Lack Of Trust

    Without clear signals of credibility, visitors hesitate to make contact or hand over their details.

  • Poor Conversion Paths

    Without a clear next step, even interested visitors leave without enquiring.
