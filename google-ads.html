Why Google Ads Works

Most advertising interrupts people who weren't looking for you. Google Ads is different — it places your business in front of people at the exact moment they're actively searching for what you offer, with clear buying or research intent already in mind.

Search Intent
Someone searching "emergency plumber near me" isn't casually browsing — they need a solution now, making search traffic some of the highest-intent traffic available.
Timing Over Interruption
Unlike social advertising, which interrupts people mid-scroll, search advertising meets people at the exact moment they've already decided to look for a solution.
Measurable From Day One
Every click, call and form submission can be tracked back to a specific keyword and campaign, giving a clear view of what's actually driving enquiries.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I budget for Google Ads?

Budget depends on your industry, competition and objective. We'll review your market during your audit and recommend a realistic starting budget based on genuine search volume and competition, not a generic figure.

How is Google Ads different from SEO?

Google Ads places you at the top of search results immediately and is paid per click, while SEO builds long-term organic visibility over months. Many businesses use both — Ads for immediate visibility, SEO for compounding growth.

How quickly can a campaign go live?

Once strategy, keywords and ad copy are agreed, campaigns can typically launch within a few days. Google's review process for ad approval usually takes 24-48 hours.

Do I need a website to run Google Ads?

Yes, in most cases. Search and Shopping campaigns need a destination page that matches the search intent, though Lead Form extensions can capture enquiries directly within some ad formats.

How do you decide which keywords to target?

We research the exact terms your customers use when they're ready to enquire or buy, prioritising commercial intent over high-volume but low-relevance searches that waste budget.

Can you manage an existing Google Ads account?

Yes. We regularly audit and take over existing accounts, identifying wasted spend and restructuring campaigns to improve lead quality and performance.
