Common Mistakes

Why Businesses Waste Ad Spend

Meta Ads can be one of the most effective growth channels available — or one of the most expensive mistakes a business makes. The difference almost always comes down to strategy, targeting and ongoing management, not budget size.

Before scaling any campaign, it's worth understanding where ad spend typically gets wasted, and why a structured approach matters more than the platform itself.

  • Broad, Untargeted Audiences

    Campaigns built for everyone reach no one efficiently, burning budget on people who were never going to convert.

  • No Creative Testing

    Running a single ad creative for weeks means missed opportunities to find what actually resonates with your audience.

  • Poor Conversion Tracking

    Without accurate pixel and event tracking, it's impossible to know which campaigns are actually driving results.

  • Ignoring Retargeting

    Most visitors don't convert on their first visit — failing to re-engage them leaves revenue on the table.

  • Set-and-Forget Campaigns

    Ad accounts left unmanaged after launch slowly become inefficient as costs rise and audiences fatigue.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much budget do I need to start with Meta Ads?

Budget requirements vary by industry and objective. We recommend a minimum that allows the algorithm to gather enough data to optimise effectively, which we'll advise on based on your specific goals during your strategy call.

How long before Meta Ads campaigns start working?

Campaigns typically need an initial learning phase of one to two weeks to gather performance data, with meaningful optimisation improvements building over the following weeks as we refine targeting and creative.

Do you manage both Facebook and Instagram ads?

Yes. Every campaign is built across Meta's platforms, including Facebook and Instagram placements, selected based on where your audience is most active.

What creative assets do I need to provide?

We can work with existing brand assets, product photography or video, and also advise on the creative direction needed to test effectively — you don't need a large asset library to get started.

How do you measure campaign success?

Success is measured against the objective agreed for each campaign — leads, sales or brand reach — using accurate conversion tracking rather than vanity metrics like impressions alone.

Can you take over an existing Meta Ads account?

Yes. We regularly audit and take over existing ad accounts, identifying inefficiencies and restructuring campaigns for improved performance.
