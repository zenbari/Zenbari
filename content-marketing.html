FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is content marketing different from just having a blog?

Content marketing is a structured strategy built around business goals and customer intent, not just regularly publishing blog posts without direction.

How long does content marketing take to work?

Content marketing is a long-term strategy. Most businesses see meaningful traction over three to six months, with results compounding over time.

Do you write the content yourselves?

Yes, we research, write and structure content ourselves, ensuring it aligns with your strategy, brand and business objectives.

How does content support SEO and AI search?

Well-structured, genuinely useful content gives search engines and AI-powered tools the clarity and relevance they need to understand and recommend your business.

What types of content do you create?

This depends on your strategy, but commonly includes website content, blog articles, guides and content designed to support email and social channels.

How do you measure content marketing success?

We track meaningful metrics like organic traffic, engagement and lead generation, rather than vanity metrics like page views alone.
